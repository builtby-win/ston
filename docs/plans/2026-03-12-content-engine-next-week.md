# Next Week Content Engine Implementation Plan

> **For Claude:** REQUIRED SUB-SKILL: Use superpowers:executing-plans to implement this plan task-by-task.

**Goal:** Publish one blog-driven content batch next week that grows audience and feeds interest into `back2vibing`.

**Architecture:** Use one blog post as the source asset, then repurpose it into one X thread, four standalone X posts, two short videos, and one build-in-public update. Keep the system simple enough to repeat weekly in 4-5 hours.

**Tech Stack:** Markdown blog in `src/content/blog/`, X, short-form video (Reels/TikTok/YouTube Shorts), screenshots/screen recordings, notes app for hooks

### Task 2: Draft the blog post

**Files:**
- Create: `src/content/blog/apps-i-use-every-day-as-an-ai-builder.md`

**Step 1: Create the frontmatter**

Use this structure:

```md
---
title: "the apps i use every day as an ai builder"
date: 2026-03-15
description: "the tools, workflows, and apps i actually use to build fast with AI"
tags: ["ai", "tooling", "workflow", "builder"]
draft: true
---
```

**Step 2: Write the intro**

Cover:
- you are building multiple apps
- you use AI tools heavily every day
- this is not a generic productivity stack
- these are the tools you actually rely on

**Step 3: List 5-7 apps**

For each app, write:
- what it is
- why you use it
- what problem it solves in your day-to-day workflow
- one personal opinion or usage detail

**Step 4: End with workflow synthesis**

Write a short section on why the stack works together.

**Step 5: Add the soft CTA**

End with a light plug for `back2vibing` as part of the workflow, not a hard sell.

**Step 6: Verify draft quality**

Checklist:
- real tools only
- real opinions only
- no fake expert fluff
- readable in 3-5 minutes

## Definition of Done for the Week

- 1 blog post drafted and published
- 1 X thread posted
- 4 standalone X posts written, with at least 3 posted
- 2 short videos scripted, recorded, and posted
- 1 build-in-public update posted
- 4 engagement sessions completed
- 1 next-week topic chosen before the week ends

## Time Budget

- Blog draft: `60 min`
- X thread + posts: `60 min`
- Video scripts: `30 min`
- Recording/editing/posting: `90 min`
- Engagement: `120 min`
- Weekly review: `20 min`

Total: about `4.5-5 hours`

## Anti-Failure Rules

- do not invent new content formats this week
- do not chase perfection on the blog draft
- do not skip the CTA on everything
- do not hard sell in every post
- if something performs well, reuse the angle next week
